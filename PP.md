Privacy Policy for Yukihana Bot
Last Updated: 14th March 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Yukihana Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you invite and use the Bot in your server. By using the Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect
To provide music streaming and utility features, Yukihana must interact with the Discord API. We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary for the Bot to function. This data may include:
 * Discord IDs: We collect and temporarily store User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, and Channel IDs.
 * Command Usage: We log the commands executed by users, including the time and the server where the command was run.
 * Music Requests: We temporarily process search queries, URLs, and playlist names provided to the Bot to fetch and stream audio content.
 * Voice Channel State: The Bot reads voice channel states to know when to join, play audio, and leave when a channel is empty.

3. How We Use Your Information
The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:
 * Core Functionality: Executing commands, managing the music queue, and joining or leaving voice channels.
 * Troubleshooting and Debugging: Identifying and fixing errors, crashes, or bugs within the Bot's code.
 * Abuse Prevention: Monitoring command usage to prevent spam, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.

4. Data Storage and Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
 * Temporary Storage: Information like active music queues and voice channel states are stored strictly in memory and are cleared when playback ends or the Bot restarts.
 * Persistent Storage: Data such as specific server configurations or blacklisted IDs are stored securely in our database.

5. Third-Party Services
Yukihana relies on third-party services to function.
 * Discord API: The Bot receives and sends data through the Discord API. Your use of the Bot is also subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
 * Audio Sources: The Bot interacts with platforms like YouTube, Spotify, or Soundcloud to fetch audio streams. We do not control the data collection practices of these external platforms.

6. Data Retention and Deletion
We retain data only for as long as it is necessary to provide our services.
 * If you remove the Bot from your server, we will cease collecting new data from that server immediately.
 * Users or Server Administrators can request the deletion of any stored configurations or data associated with their ID or Server ID by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated through the Bot's official support channels. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
